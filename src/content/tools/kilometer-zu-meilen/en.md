## What This Tool Does

The kilometer is the standard unit of distance in nearly every country outside the United States. American travelers, runners following global training plans, and anyone buying or renting a vehicle abroad routinely need to convert between kilometers and miles. This converter handles the calculation instantly and includes a reference table for the most common distances.

## How Does It Work?

**Miles = Kilometers × 0.621371192**

The exact definition: 1 international mile = 1,609.344 meters (exact, since 1959). Therefore:

1 km = 1,000 m ÷ 1,609.344 m/mi = 0.6213711922... mi

## What Are Common Use Cases?

- **Road trips in Canada or Mexico:** Canadian highways use km/h and km distance markers. Convert to miles to understand the distance from your US mental map.
- **Running and cycling:** Most international races, Garmin/Strava workouts, and global training plans use kilometers. Convert to miles to compare your pace or race goal.
- **Imported vehicles:** European and Japanese cars often have km/h speedometers. Convert to understand how fast you're going relative to US speed limits in mph.
- **Google Maps and navigation apps:** Apps default to km in many international contexts. Set a specific km destination or distance, then convert to understand it in familiar miles.
- **Shipping and logistics:** International freight documents list distances in km for routing and fuel calculations. Convert to miles for US-based cost models.
- **Real estate:** Rural land listings in some countries reference property distances and boundaries in km. Convert for comparison with US land in acres and miles.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is there an exact conversion between km and miles?**
Yes. The international mile is defined as exactly 1,609.344 meters, making the conversion ratio exact: 1 km = 1,000/1,609.344 miles = 0.62137119223733... miles. This converter uses this exact ratio.

**Why does the US still use miles instead of kilometers?**
The US uses the US customary system, which descends from English imperial units. The Metric Conversion Act of 1975 allowed but did not mandate metric adoption in everyday life. Road infrastructure, vehicle odometers, and cultural familiarity with miles kept the US on customary units for road travel.

**How far is 1 mile in km exactly?**
1 mile = 1.609344 km exactly. For a quick mental check: a mile is about 1.6 km, and a km is about 0.6 miles.

**What is a nautical mile and how does it compare?**
A nautical mile is 1.852 km exactly (1.15078 statute miles). It is used in aviation and maritime navigation. Convert using the Nautical Miles to Km tool linked in related tools.
