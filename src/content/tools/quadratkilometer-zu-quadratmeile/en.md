## How It Works

The conversion is a direct multiplication:

**mi² = km² × 0.386102159**

The factor derives from the definition of the international mile (1 mile = 1.609344 km), squared:

1 mi² = (1.609344)² km² = 2.58998811 km²

Therefore: 1 km² = 1 / 2.58998811 mi² = 0.386102159 mi²

The tool applies the full-precision factor and rounds the display to 3–6 significant figures depending on the input magnitude.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does km² compare to acres?** 1 km² = 247.105 acres. For small land areas, acres are often more intuitive for US users than square miles (which are large units). This converter focuses on km² to mi²; for hectares to acres, use our dedicated tool.

**What is the difference between a square mile and a mile square?** They mean the same thing: an area equal to a square with sides 1 mile long. In the US Public Land Survey System, a "section" is 1 square mile = 640 acres.

**Why do some sources give area in km² and others in mi²?** Most of the world uses the SI metric system, so scientific and international sources default to km². The US, UK (historically), and a few other countries use square miles in everyday contexts. This converter bridges both conventions.

**Is the conversion exact?** The international mile is defined as exactly 1.609344 km, making the derived factor (0.386102159...) a repeating decimal. The tool uses floating-point arithmetic with sufficient precision for any practical measurement.
